ReaWave is a company inspired by ocean pollution and how to solve it, one bead at a time. Each of our jewelry and clothing peices are made from recyled plastics and metals. Our story started with learning about the cruelties of plastic in 1st grade, what seemed like a small step, quickly turned into big ideas! We hope to change lives, not just of our own species, but also of the increadible animals that inhabit over 75% of the earths globe. We hope to save these animals and ourselves, one bead at a time, it would be an honour to have you support us on our journey!
